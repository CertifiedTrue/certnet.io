What is CertNet?

Scalable, Modular Infrastructure
for dApps and Smart Contracts.

Certnet is a next generation blockchain network and smart contract platform.

While existing solutions offer to solve just one problem at a time, our team is building a secure, useful, & easy-to-use service based on public/private blockchain consortium. It will include easy cryptocurrency payments integration, and a digital arbitration system.

Public-Private Hybrid

Consortium Blockchain Network

CertifiedTrue is a public/private consortium effort created to advance blockchain certification in Legal, Insurance and Real Estate (LIR) industries. It is a composition of blockchain, smart contracts, web3js, content addressable storage and other enterprise technologies. Certnet the blockchain is fostered by CertifiedTrue who provides blockchain business certification services, ecosystem and community management.
